How to Deploy Your Portfolio

Hereâ€™s how you can deploy your portfolio online with popular platforms:

1. Deploying with GitHub Pages

  1. Push your portfolio code to a GitHub repository.
  2. Go to your repositoryâ€™s settings.
  3. Scroll down to the "GitHub Pages" section.
  4. Select "main" branch and click "Save".
  5. Your portfolio will be available at https://username.github.io/repository-name/

2. Deploying with Netlify

  1. Sign up for a free account at Netlify.
  2. Click on the "New Site from Git" button.
  3. Choose GitHub as your provider and authorize Netlify.
  4. Select the repository containing your portfolio code.
  5. Click on "Deploy Site".
  6. Your portfolio will be live on a Netlify URL (e.g., https://your-site-name.netlify.app/).

3. Deploying with Vercel

  1. Sign up for a free account at Vercel.
  2. Click on the "New Project" button.
  3. Link your GitHub account and select the repository with your portfolio.
  4. Click "Deploy".
  5. Your portfolio will be available on a Vercel URL (e.g., https://your-project-name.vercel.app/).

All of these platforms provide free hosting, and they automatically deploy when you push new changes to your repository!
